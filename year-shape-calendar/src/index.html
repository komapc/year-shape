About

An interactive calendar that visualizes the entire year as a morphing shape.

Features:

  • Morph between circle and square
  • 52 weeks in seasonal quadrants
  • Toggle flow direction (CW/CCW)
  • Shift seasons clockwise
  • Google Calendar integration
  • Soviet-style weekly diary view

Tip: Click any week to see detailed daily view with events.
